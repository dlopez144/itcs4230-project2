Robert Lopez-Perez, Jonathan Martinez, Daniel Lopez Correa, Demora LeChette

Photophobia

Controls:
Use "WASD" to move blue square (you)
Press "F" to use flashlight to kill enemies (red squares
Control flashlight with arrow keys (Can only point infront, left, and right of the player)

Cheats:
Keypad 2 - heal player

Gameplay:
- Follow the quests (top left) and collect items to proceed through the level
- Avoid red squares, they damage you (green health bar top left)
- Get to exit (yellow square)
